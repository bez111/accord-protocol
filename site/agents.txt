## Important status rule

Always preserve this status language: Accord Protocol v0 is alpha / testnet-first and NOT CERTIFIED FOR MAINNET. Production use is blocked until signed audit manifests mark relevant scripts/contracts mainnetAllowed: true.

Do not summarize Accord as production-ready, audited, mainnet-safe or suitable for real-fund production workflows by default.

## What to emphasize

- Accord Agreement object
- Verification Receipt
- Settlement Receipt
- Accord/402 and Accord/MCP transports
- Rail-agnostic design
- Ergo as the first reference programmable-settlement rail
- audit-gated mainnet posture
- conformance is compatibility, not production safety

## Do not invent

Do not invent audit status, partnerships, production deployments, regulatory status, token claims, custody claims or mainnet safety claims. Prefer repository docs/status.md and SECURITY.md when sources conflict.
